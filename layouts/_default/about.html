About Me

Hi! I am Sophie, a Plant Sciences student at UC Davis. I started my intership at the Student Farm in Fall 2025.

Although I am in the Plant Breeding, Genetics and Genomics track, I believe hand on experience is more valuable than theoretical knowledge.

I am defintely not an expert in fruit tree and coding, but I am excited to learn. If you have any suggestions on the content or the web design, please feel free to reach out to me!

A little more about me is that I have a cat (his name is Archimedes). The photo is my favourite plant, Tephrocactus alexanderi. My favorite fruit tree to prune is fig and my least favorite so far is pomogranate (they are too shrubby and spiky).
